Ethical Decision-Making Framework

Walk through a structured process to analyse AI ethical scenarios and generate a documented decision.

← Back to Course Hub

Step 1: Define the Scenario

Describe the AI-related decision or scenario you want to analyse, or select a sample scenario below.

Step 2: Identify Stakeholders

List all individuals and groups who could be affected by this decision. Consider direct users, indirect beneficiaries, vulnerable populations, and society at large.

Step 3: Assess Risks & Benefits

For the scenario described, identify the potential benefits and risks for each stakeholder group.

Step 4: Apply Ethical Principles

Select which ethical principles are relevant to this scenario and note how they apply.

Step 5: Consider Alternatives

What alternative approaches could better satisfy the ethical requirements? Think about different technical approaches, safeguards, or process changes.

Step 6: Make Your Decision

Based on your analysis, what is the most ethically sound course of action? Document your reasoning.
