Utilitarian

Cost-Effectiveness

Principle: Greatest good for the greatest number.

AI Stance: Allocate resources to those with the highest expected benefit per dollar spent.

✓ Maximises efficiency and total outcomes
✗ Risks excluding vulnerable, high-cost patients

🤝

Egalitarian

Equal Access

Principle: Equal right to resources, regardless of cost.

AI Stance: Distribute resources equally; strictly forbid decisions based on ability-to-pay.

✓ Maximises equity and fairness
✗ Undermines cost optimisation and sustainability

🎯

Prioritarian

Needs-Based

Principle: Prioritise the worst off.

AI Stance: Include a "vulnerability index" to boost rankings for the most severely ill or disadvantaged.

✓ Bridges the profit-access divide
✗ Requires subsidisation; complex to implement
