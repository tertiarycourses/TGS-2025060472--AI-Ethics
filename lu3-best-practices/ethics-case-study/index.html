Cross-Cutting Patterns

Power Asymmetry

AI systems often concentrate decision-making power, reducing the agency of those affected by decisions.

Feedback Loops

Biased AI decisions create biased data, which trains more biased models — a self-reinforcing cycle.

Accountability Gaps

Complex AI supply chains make it difficult to assign responsibility when harm occurs.

Consent Erosion

Data collected for one purpose is repurposed for AI training without meaningful consent.

Digital Divide

Benefits of AI accrue to those with resources and access, widening existing inequalities.

Transparency Theatre

Publishing model cards or ethics statements without meaningful accountability or change.
